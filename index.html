Hands Free for Chrome
A Chrome extension to browse the web using only your voice, free to download and use. A mic is required.

Hands Free for Chrome allows the user to do nearly everything they could normally, without ever touching a mouse or keyboard.

You can scroll around, click things, navigate to particular webpages, go back or forward in your browsing history, enter text into forms, check and uncheck checkboxes, search pages for text, zoom in and out, open and close tabs, refresh the page, and more. This is an initial release, and many improvements and additional features will soon follow.
