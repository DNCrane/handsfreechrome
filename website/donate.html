Hands Free for Chrome
A Chrome extension to browse the web using only your voice, free to download and use. A mic is required.

I started building this out of want for a project, but before long it became clear how much Hands Free could help those who would otherwise be unable to do things like peruse the news or search for information.

If you would like to make a donation to express your support and encourage further development, that would certainly be appreciated, and you can do so via this handy donations-accepting page.
